About

I am a PhD student in Robotics, focusing on mobile robot navigation, simultaneous localization and mapping (SLAM), and odometry. My research aims to develop robust, efficient algorithms that enable robots to navigate complex environments with minimal computational overhead.

I value clean system design, reproducible results, and open-source tooling. My background spans embedded systems, sensor fusion, and real-time control.
